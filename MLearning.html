Unit1-3

Collaborative Discussion

The 4th Industrial Revolution is happening faster than ever before. One factor is that mobile devices now connect billions of people and can collect and access vast amounts of data. As a result, It has a disruptive impact on almost all industries and has the potential to raise global income levels and improve the quality of life of people worldwide (Schwab, K., 2016). On the other hand, the problems caused by information system failures related to data are numerous and cause serious problems. Toyota, one of Japan's largest companies, has revealed that it had leaked the vehicle details of more than two million of its Japanese customers since 2012 (Leussink, D., Komiya, K., 2023). It also affected Toyota's luxury car Lexus. It is an incredibly popular car and an easy target for thieves. In certain areas of Japan, data shows that 20 of the Lexus LX SUVs models in the area have been stolen (Fujimaki, K., Ko, A., 2021). This fact leads us to believe that the leaked data was in the hands of thieves, who were stealing it. As assets exchanged in electronic data, cryptocurrencies are easy targets for hackers. North Korea stole a total of USD 2.3 billion in virtual currency from companies between 2017 and 2022, an investigation found (Reuters Staff, 2023). The huge outflows of assets lead to a loss of trust in the cryptocurrency itself. By and large, the sheer volume of data has made people's lives so much richer. However, It is important to remember that mishandling of data can cause serious problems. People who work with data need to think carefully about how they deal with data.
